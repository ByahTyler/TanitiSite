Travel Information

Everything you need to plan your trip

Getting to Taniti
  • Accessible by small aircraft and regional flights
  • Airport expansion planned for larger jets
  • Weekly cruise ship docking at Yellow Leaf Bay
  • Harbor supports private boats
Getting Around
  • Public buses run 5 a.m. - 11 p.m.
  • Taxis available throughout Taniti City
  • Rental cars near the airport
  • Bikes available (helmets required)
Entry Information
  • No special visa required for most visitors
  • Passport recommended
  • Customs checks upon arrival
Travel Tips
  • Bring light clothing for tropical weather
  • Stay hydrated and use sunscreen
  • Respect local customs and environment

Important Information

  • Power outlets are 120 volts (U.S. standard)
  • Currency: U.S. dollar (euros & yen accepted)
  • Major credit cards widely accepted
  • English widely spoken in tourist areas
  • Drinking age is 18
  • No alcohol sales between midnight - 9 a.m.
  • Hospital and clinics available
  • Low violent crime; watch for pickpocketing
