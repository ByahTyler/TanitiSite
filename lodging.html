Taniti offers a wide variety of lodging options, from inexpensive hostels to a large four-star resort. Many small, family-owned hotels and bed and breakfasts provide a more personal experience.

All lodging options are strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government to ensure safety and quality.
